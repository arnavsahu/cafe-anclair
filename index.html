Our Story

Born from a sisterly bond
& a shared dream

Sisters Ann and Claire grew up on Mercer Island imagining conventional careers — desk jobs, eight-hour days. But when they discovered that life wasn't for them, an unexpected spark changed everything. Their parents casually mentioned a coffee shop, and a new dream took root.

Ann found her joy in the kitchen. Claire became an artisanal coffee connoisseur. Through this complementary pairing, Café An'Clair was born in 2018 — combining their names to create a space uniquely their own. Now with three locations and a loyal community, they blend craft, Korean heritage, and genuine human connection in every cup.

— Ann & Claire
